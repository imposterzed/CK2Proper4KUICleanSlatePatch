# Getting Support

Looking for help with Proper 4K UI Project - CleanSlate Patch? Here's where to go.

## Bug reports

Open an [issue](../../issues/new/choose) and pick the **Bug report**
template. Include your mod version, mod stack, and any relevant
`setup.log` snippets showing which mods loaded.

## "How do I..." questions

Please use [Discussions](../../discussions) rather than opening an
issue. Discussions is the right space for installation help, mod-stack
recommendations, and general modding chat.

## CK2 modding community

For broader CK2 modding questions not specific to Proper 4K UI Project -
CleanSlate Patch, the [Paradox CK2 forums](https://forum.paradoxplaza.com/forum/forums/crusader-kings-ii.749/)
remain the long-standing community space.
